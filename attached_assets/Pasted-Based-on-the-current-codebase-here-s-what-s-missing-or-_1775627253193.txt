Based on the current codebase, here's what's missing or could improve NyumbaHub:

### 🔴 Missing / Not Working
1. **Payment INSERT RLS policy** — landlords can't record payments (only SELECT policy exists)
2. **Tenant creation for unlinked units** — no RLS policy lets owners insert tenants without a unit
3. **Forgot/Reset password UI** — no pages exist despite being mentioned in docs
4. **Email verification flow** — no confirmation page after signup
5. **Auto-confirm is off** — users can't sign in until email verified, but there's no UX guidance for this

### 🟡 Functional Gaps
6. **Lease renewal alerts** — no notification when leases are expiring soon
7. **Overdue invoice auto-marking** — no cron/trigger to flip `pending` → `overdue` past due date
8. **Receipt generation** — tenants can't download payment receipts
9. **Document/file uploads** — lease documents, ID copies, maintenance photos (no Storage bucket)
10. **Bulk SMS** — rent reminders to all tenants with overdue invoices
11. **Dashboard search/filter** — no global search, no filtering on most tables
12. **Pagination** — all tables fetch everything, will break at scale

### 🟢 UX Improvements
13. **Onboarding wizard** — guide new landlords through adding first property → unit → tenant → invoice
14. **Dark mode toggle** — design tokens exist but no theme switcher
15. **Notification center** — in-app notifications for new payments, maintenance requests, messages
16. **Tenant invite flow** — email invite with magic link instead of manual share
17. **Mobile-responsive tables** — current tables likely overflow on the 759px viewport
18. **Multi-property dashboard** — filter overview KPIs by property
19. **Expense tracking** — landlords need to track costs (repairs, taxes, management fees) not just income
20. **Occupancy rate trends** — historical chart, not just current snapshot
21. **Bulk actions** — select multiple invoices to mark paid, send reminders
22. **Export options** — PDF reports, not just CSV
23. **Audit log viewer** — table exists but no UI to view it
24. **Role-based nav** — accountant/viewer roles exist in enum but no differentiated access

### 🔵 Advanced / Growth
25. **Multi-language (i18n)** — Swahili, French for Rwanda
26. **WhatsApp notifications** — via WhatsApp Business API
27. **Tenant rating/review system**
28. **Automated rent escalation** — annual % increase per lease terms
29. **Tax reports** — withholding tax, VAT summaries for KRA compliance
30. **Caretaker/agent sub-accounts** — manager role with property-scoped access
